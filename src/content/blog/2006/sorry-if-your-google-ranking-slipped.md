pubDate: '2006-07-13'
description: 'A short account of being accused of harming someone else Google ranking, requiring a moment of genuine reflection before concluding that no individual blogger has any influence over how Google ranks pages.'

Like it says in the title, I'm sorry if I have unintentionally harmed your Google ranking. No I'm not being silly, I was actually accused of this quite recently. I actually had to stop and think how to respond to that statement. In the end I didn't really apologize. I simply said that I had no influence upon how Google ranks. I wish I did.
