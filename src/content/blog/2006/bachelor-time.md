Well, the wife and kids are packed up and shipped off to the islands for the rest of the summer. I miss them already. For better or worse it seems that we are all taking a little less holiday this summer. The good news is that means I'll only be without them for about a month instead of a month and a half. I know they'll have a great time with their grandparents, aunts, uncles, and cousins.
