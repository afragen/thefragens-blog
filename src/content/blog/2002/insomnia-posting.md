I'm sure that no one is really taking note that most of my blog posts are at night. What can I say. You get free time when you get free time. The days are usually pretty busy either in the office, in the hospital or in the operating room. Yes, I am a surgeon. Now seems to be the only "quiet" time. My wife and kids are all asleep. Unfortunately for me the mind races and insomnia kicks in.
