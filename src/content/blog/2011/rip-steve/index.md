It appears that [Steve Jobs](http://www.apple.com/stevejobs/) has finally lost his long battle with pancreatic cancer. I am saddened by the loss as Steve has greatly enriched my life through his creativity and genius.  
There are very few people in the world that have enriched the lives of so many. We shall miss you Steve, but we'll never forget you.
