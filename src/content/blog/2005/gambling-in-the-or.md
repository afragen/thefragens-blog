In the morning I'm going to do what can best be described as "playing the odds." At worse, a statistical guess. 

I'm never wild about operating on a nuclear medicine bleeding scan, but you do what you've gotta do. Somehow most nuclear medicine scans look like dust has settled on the film and the what you're left with the negative image of that. 

Anyway, I always keep my fingers crossed that the bleeding is stopped after I get done. Those pesky AVMs.
