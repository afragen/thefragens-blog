Well if you're reading this at radio.weblogs.com them you've probably realized that I've flipped the switch. Well, maybe you haven't. Other than a small change to the layout and no calendar you shouldn't notice any real changes. I guess that's the whole point. 

If [Squib](http://squib.rubyforge.org/) is working properly then you won't really be able to tell that I'm not using [Radio][2] at the moment. If you're reasonably geeky and are getting tired of the persistent difficulties in using Radio. I may have an option for you. Just ask.
