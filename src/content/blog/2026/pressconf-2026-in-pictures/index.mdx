Shot with Leica M11, Summilux-M 1:1.4/35 ASPH, and Apo-Summicron-M 1:2/75 ASPH. Most photos were unedited. A few images had adjustments made to exposure, highlights, and shadows to enhance the overall look of the images. I'm still getting used to focusing on the rangefinder, but that's just part of the style.
