Jeff is someone I consider a friend. When I made my last post about Kerry's discharge he emailed me. We've been carrying on a rather lengthy discussion of the issues. All without the usual ad hominem attacks on either candidate. It's refreshing. Perhaps we'll find a way to publish it, but then to what real point. We both understand where we're coming from and I'm not sure that (YAPBP) Yet Another Political Blog Post is worth cluttering up Google. I'll actually be very happy when all this is over. 

I really don't like the way some of the people whose blogs and software I read and use don't seem to put much thought of their own into some of the political posts that they make. Don't look to hard, there are no links here.
