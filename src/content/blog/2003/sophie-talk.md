My daughter, Sophie, is a bit older than 2. She's really starting to form multiple word sentences and speak in complete thoughts. I really wish I understood the language that she's using. She seems so serious when she speaks and I can't help but be in rapt attention.

Hopefully, someday soon, all this babbling will turn into something resembling fluent English. But I do have to say that she is able to communicate her points exactly.
