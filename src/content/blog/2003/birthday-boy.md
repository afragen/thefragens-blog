pubDate: '2003-03-11'
description: 'Son Jonathan is turning eight and has been given a motivating challenge: reach reading and writing fluency and earn a new iMac connected to the home wireless network.'
categories: ['family']
---

Jonathan will be 8 years old on Saturday. He's getting bigger, smarter, and more perceptive all the time. I've given him some incentive with his reading fluency. When he gets to the point that he's very capable writing, typing, and reading I'll get him a new iMac. That way I can hook him into our wireless LAN and give him net access and access to the laser printer. Currently the 7200/90 and StyleWriter occupy a larger percentage of his desktop.
