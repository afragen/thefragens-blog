We've been busy expanding to a new space in the office building. I lost the battle to actually have an office room in my dad's space but I will win the war. And someone will lose their job -- just not yet. So I get an office in the new space. So does Mom. I'm actually considering moving my general surgery office to this new space and leaving my present office in RM.

There are many advantages to the move and I'll have to look into when my lease is up.
