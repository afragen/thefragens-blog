PressConf is the reincarnation of Pressnomics. It's a non-recorded, single track conference where you have to be in the room. While recordings were not allowed, photos were encouraged.

I took the opportunity to record some memories. B&W pics are only in B&W. I only shot with a fixed 35mm lens. I did a light pass over the images with cropping and some post-processing in [Photomater](https://www.pixelmator.com/photomator/). I'm still getting used to using a rangefinder camera and the focus is much different than the SLRs I'm used to using.

Enjoy!
